The SHOULD Problem

It depends.

First-time authors are drowning in SHOULD-questions that don't have universal answers.

Should I publish just on Amazon or go wide?

Just Amazon is perfectly fine for 99% of authors.

Should I use a free Amazon ISBN or buy my own?

The free ISBN from Amazon is perfectly fine.

What trim size should I use?

6"×9" — unless you have a specific reason not to.

How much should I spend on editing?

Depends on your goals and budget. Probably less than you think.

How much should I spend on cover design?

Depends on your goals and budget. Probably less than you think.

Two authors can have identical goals and completely different right answers based on budget alone. It's not a this-or-that decision. It's a matrix. And nobody online is treating it like one.

A less than perfect published book is better than a never-published book. Every time.
